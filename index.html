Who It's For

Different stakes.
Same need for verified geometry.

For Engineers & Designers
Iterate thermal design on real geometry, not assumptions.
  • Confirm installed spacing and verticality match your model
  • Feed actual borefield geometry back into your thermal design — not initial theoretical values
  • Specify verification on dense or boundary-adjacent projects
For Project Owners
Know exactly what's underground — and whose property it's on.
  • Verify bores stayed within your property boundary
  • Subsurface as-built documentation for your long-term asset records
  • Verification report for commissioning and regulatory handover
For Drilling Contractors
Add borefield verification to your service offering.
  • New differentiated service line for geothermal projects
  • Assistant driller can easily deploy on a standard line
  • Deliver professional verification reports to engineering clients
